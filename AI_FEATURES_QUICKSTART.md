# 🚀 AI Features Quick Start

## ✨ What's New?

We've added two incredible AI-powered features to DSA Orbit:

### 1. **AI Quiz Generator** 📝
- Generate smart quizzes based on your completed DSA topics
- Multiple difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard)
- Detailed explanations for every question
- Performance grading and score analytics

**Access:** Click on the navbar "✨ AI Quiz" button

### 2. **AI Doubt Solver** 🤖
- ChatGPT-like interface for solving DSA doubts
- Ask any DSA question and get instant explanations
- Multiple hint modes (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced)
- Personalized recommendations based on your progress

**Access:** Click on the navbar "🤖 AI Doubt" button

---

## 🎮 How to Use

### AI Quiz Generator
1. Select your completed topics (Arrays, Strings, Recursion, Sorting)
2. Choose difficulty level
3. Click "Generate AI Quiz"
4. Answer all 5 questions
5. Review your score and explanations
6. Regenerate for more practice

### AI Doubt Solver
1. Open the chatbot interface
2. Ask any DSA question
3. AI responds with step-by-step explanation
4. Toggle hint mode for different explanation levels
5. Use quick suggestions to explore topics
6. Check your progress on the sidebar
